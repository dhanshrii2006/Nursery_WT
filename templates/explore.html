How often should I water my rescue plant?

There's no single answer! The best way is the "finger test." Stick your finger about an inch into the soil. If it feels dry, it's time to water. If it's damp, wait a few more days. Overwatering is the most common way to kill a houseplant, so it's better to be a little too dry than too wet.

What does "bright, indirect light" actually mean?

Imagine the spot where you can comfortably read a book during the day without needing a lamp. That's bright, indirect light. It's usually found near an east-facing window or a few feet back from a sunny south or west-facing window. Direct sunbeams can scorch the leaves of many indoor plants.
